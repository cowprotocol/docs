# CoW Grants Program Overview

The CoW Grants Program (CGP) funds initiatives that support the growth of the CoW Protocol ecosystem, aligned with the [CoW DAO mission statement](/governance/mission).

## Current Mandate

The Grants Program operates under [CIP-82](https://snapshot.box/#/s:cow.eth/proposal/0xe84793f122f1c9faa2bb9194faf71567ffd236df616bd60c300eead541f25304) ([forum](https://forum.cow.fi/t/cip-82-cow-dao-grants-2026-renewal-ci-cd/3280)), approved in January 2026, which provides:

- A total budget of 500,000 xDAI and approximately 6,550,314 COW
- A continued reservation of 4,990,000 COW for volume incentives
- A transition to quarterly fund releases to improve capital efficiency
- Migration of the Grants Treasury Safe to Ethereum mainnet
- Operating authority through January 31, 2027

## Strategic Focus Areas

The CoW Grants Program focuses on these key areas:

- **Developer Onboarding and Enablement**: Creating pathways for developers to understand and build on CoW through improved documentation, SDKs, and direct support.
- **Strategic Ecosystem Alignment**: Partnering with external protocols where CoW provides distinct advantages around MEV protection and batch auctions.
- **Novel Applications on CoW**: Supporting programmatic orders, CoW AMM implementations, and cross-chain solutions beyond basic swaps.
- **Volume Incentive Program**: Maintaining a reserved allocation of COW for deployment when partner activity demonstrates clear value multiplication.

## Committee Structure

CIP-82 established a five-member committee (three community, two core team) with a 3/5 signing threshold to prevent core team veto. Following the departure of one core team member in March 2026, the committee currently operates with four members and a 3/4 threshold. The committee combines technical, operational, and strategic expertise to evaluate proposals and oversee the grant lifecycle.

The program emphasizes outcome-based evaluation, tracking success through metrics such as volume growth, developer adoption, and infrastructure improvements.
