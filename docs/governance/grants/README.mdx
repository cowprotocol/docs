# CoW Grants Program Overview

The CoW Grants Program (CGP) funds initiatives that support the growth of the CoW Protocol ecosystem, aligned with the [CoW DAO mission statement](/governance/mission).

## Current Mandate

The Grants Program operates under [CIP-82](https://snapshot.box/#/s:cow.eth/proposal/0xe84793f122f1c9faf71567ffd236df616bd60c300eead541f25304), approved in January 2026, which provides:

- A total budget of 500,000 xDAI and approximately 6,550,314 COW
- A continued reservation of 4,990,000 COW for volume incentives
- A transition to quarterly fund releases to improve capital efficiency
- Migration of the Grants Treasury Safe to Ethereum mainnet
- Operating authority through January 31, 2027

## Strategic Focus Areas

The CoW Grants Program focusses on these key areas:

- **Developer Onboarding**: Creating pathways via improved documentation, SDKs, and "Playground" infrastructure.
- **Strategic Alignment**: Partnering with protocols that benefit from MEV protection and batch auctions.
- **Novel Applications**: Supporting programmatic orders, CoW AMMs, and cross-chain solutions.
- **Ecosystem Growth**: Increasing network resilience, solver diversity, and active user engagement.
- **Integrations**: Establishing deep partnerships to increase protocol order flow.

## Committee Structure

The Grants Committee consists of four members (three community members and one core team member) who operate through a multisig with a 3/4 signing threshold. The committee combines technical, operational, and strategic expertise to evaluate proposals and oversee the grant lifecycle.

The program emphasizes outcome-based evaluation, tracking success through metrics such as volume growth, developer adoption, and infrastructure improvements.
